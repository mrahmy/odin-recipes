Greek Salad Recipe

Salads should not be something that only the most disciplined people endulge in. Here is a way to make a salad enjoyable for everyone.

First you need to have a base. We will use romaine lettuce as the base. Next we will add some flavorful toppings. Toppings will include different flavors.

  • olives
  • feta cheese
  • greek dressing

Steps

  1. pour the base into the bowl
  2. add olives
  3. add feta cheese
  4. add dressing
